# Growth Product Examples

## Product Bet

```markdown
Product candidate: Local AI video workflow pack.
Evidence: Monthly review names AI video as a strategic opportunity.
Assumption: Creator workflow demand is stronger than current direct revenue evidence.
Validation: Publish one landing page and measure qualified clicks before building a full product.
```

## Revenue Validation

```markdown
Experiment: Add a low-friction preorder CTA to one product page.
Success metric: Qualified checkout or contact intent.
Risk: Revenue source evidence is partial.
```

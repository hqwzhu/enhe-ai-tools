# AI Digital Product Launcher

## Purpose
Package an AI product idea into launch-ready copy, checklist, marketplace listing drafts, and Codex tasks.

## When to Use
- The user wants to launch an AI digital product.
- ENHE needs product page copy, FAQ, SEO/GEO labels, or marketplace copy.
- A product opportunity needs a launch checklist.
- Codex needs product page tasks from a validated idea.

## Required Inputs
- EBOS evidence: none required for an early launch draft.
- Optional evidence: `product_evidence`, `seo_evidence`, `geo_evidence`.
- Project files: product page patterns, pricing, content guidelines, media assets.
- Human input: product idea, target buyer, offer, price, delivery method.

## Process
1. Clarify product promise, buyer, use case, deliverables, and proof.
2. Read available product, SEO, and GEO evidence.
3. Draft launch checklist, product page copy, FAQ, and marketplace copy.
4. Define cover image and demo asset requirements.
5. Identify missing evidence and validation risks.
6. Generate Codex product page tasks only when implementation is authorized.

## Output Format
Return Markdown with:
- Product launch checklist.
- Product page copy.
- Marketplace listing copy.
- FAQ.
- SEO/GEO label suggestions.
- Cover image requirements.
- Codex product page tasks.

## Quality Checklist
- Offer and buyer are explicit.
- Evidence gaps are marked.
- Copy does not overpromise.
- SEO/GEO labels are supported by product facts.
- Tasks are implementation-ready.

## Guardrails
- Do not claim product capabilities that are not provided.
- Do not fabricate testimonials, sales, or performance.
- Do not output secrets.
- Do not modify product pages unless asked.
- Mark confidence when product evidence is missing.

## Example Prompts
- Use EBOS ai-digital-product-launcher to launch an AI workflow pack.
- Draft product page and marketplace copy for an AI prompt kit.
- Create Codex product page tasks for a new AI digital product.

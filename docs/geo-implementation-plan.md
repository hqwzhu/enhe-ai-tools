# ENHE AI GEO Implementation Plan

## Goal

GEO means Generative Engine Optimization. For ENHE AI, the goal is to make public pages easier for Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and other answer engines to understand, quote, and connect back to useful site content.

## First-Stage Rules

中文执行关键词：答案式区块、内部链接、结构化数据、可引用段落、下一步行动。

1. Every important public page should include answer-style blocks that explain what the page is, who it helps, how to use it, and what the next useful action is.
2. Each answer block should be written in direct language, avoid slogans, and answer a real user question in one short paragraph.
3. Important pages should contain internal links to related AI news, software, account service guidance, skill-learning courses, and tutorials.
4. AI account service content must stay compliance-safe: avoid risky wording such as official recharge, shared account, cracked, bypass, no-ban guarantee, black card, or permanent availability.
5. Thin list pages should not rely only on filters and cards. They should add crawlable context, examples, and decision guidance.
6. Detail pages should keep one H1, useful H2 sections, FAQ schema when available, breadcrumb schema, canonical URLs, and descriptive image alt text.

## Page Priorities

- `/skill-learning`: learning path, suitable audience, task-to-outcome guidance, and links to trends, software, and account service support.
- `/software`: local deployment, productivity workflows, suitable use cases, pricing or access guidance, and tutorial links.
- `/account-services`: subscription and account usage support, compliance reminders, delivery boundaries, and related tool links.
- `/ai-news`: what changed, why it matters, what users can do next, and links to related tools, tutorials, and services.
- `/ai-trends`: demand signals, ranking methodology, opportunity interpretation, and links to AI news and product categories.

## Content Pattern

Use this structure when adding or refreshing GEO sections:

- What this page answers.
- Who should use this information.
- How to apply it in a real AI workflow.
- What ENHE AI page to visit next.
- What caution or compliance boundary matters.

## Ongoing Actions

1. Add answer-style blocks to every listing page before expanding to lower-priority detail pages.
2. Convert high-value AI news articles into related tool, course, and tutorial recommendations.
3. Keep English content genuinely localized, not only translated navigation.
4. Use analytics and Search Console query data to decide the next article, software listing, service page, or skill course.
5. Refresh outdated pages when AI policies, platform access rules, or model capabilities change.
